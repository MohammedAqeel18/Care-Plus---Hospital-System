Our Services

Blood Test

Blood tests are available at Care Plus Hospital from 01:00 PM to 06:00 PM every day. Comprehensive blood analysis includes CBC (Complete Blood Count), glucose levels, cholesterol tests, and liver function tests. Price starts at $20 (LKR 7,200). Additional specialized tests are available, priced individually.

Urine Test

Urine tests are available at Care Plus Hospital from 01:00 PM to 06:00 PM every day. Tests include UTI (Urinary Tract Infection) screening, protein level checks, and pregnancy confirmation. Price starts at $15 (LKR 5,400). Specialized analysis such as creatinine and microalbumin testing are available upon request.

X-ray Test

X-ray tests are available at Care Plus Hospital from 01:00 PM to 06:00 PM every day. Digital imaging is provided for chest, abdomen, and orthopedic scans. Price starts at $50 (LKR 18,000). Additional specialized X-rays such as contrast studies are available at varying prices.

MRI Test

MRI tests are available at Care Plus Hospital from 01:00 PM to 06:00 PM every day. Advanced imaging includes brain, spine, and joint scans. Price starts at $200 (LKR 72,000). Contrast-enhanced MRI scans are also offered for enhanced diagnostic accuracy at additional costs.
